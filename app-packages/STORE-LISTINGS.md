# Store Listings Guide for Multi Land

This document provides template text and guidelines for your app store listings. Customize these as needed to match your specific offerings and brand voice.

## App Name

"Multi Land" or "Multi Land - Learning Platform"

## Short Description (80 characters max)

Learn courses in multiple languages with interactive content and personalized learning.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Multi Land is a modern learning platform that delivers personalized educational experiences in multiple languages.**

Our platform offers a diverse range of courses with interactive content including videos, PDFs, text lessons, and quizzes. Whether you're looking to expand your professional skills or explore new subjects, Multi Land provides high-quality educational content in English, Russian, and Georgian.

**Key Features:**

• **Multilingual Support**: Access all content in English, Russian, and Georgian
• **Diverse Course Library**: Explore courses across various categories
• **Interactive Learning**: Engage with videos, PDFs, quizzes, and text lessons
• **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your advancement through each course
• **Flexible Payment Options**: Pay in full or choose installment plans
• **Mobile Optimization**: Learn on any device with our responsive design
• **Offline Access**: Download content for learning without internet connection
• **Personal Dashboard**: Track your enrolled courses and progress

Multi Land makes learning accessible and convenient, allowing you to acquire new skills and knowledge at your own pace, in your preferred language.

Start your learning journey today with Multi Land!

## Screenshots Description Guide

Prepare screenshots for these key app areas:

1. **Home Screen**: Show the course categories and featured courses
2. **Course Detail**: Display a course page with description and modules
3. **Content Viewer**: Show a lesson with mixed content types (video, text)
4. **My Learning**: Display the user's enrolled courses and progress
5. **Multiple Languages**: Show the language selection feature
6. **Checkout**: Display the payment options (minus sensitive information)

2. For feature updates: Create compelling release notes
3. For major redesigns: Update all screenshots and descriptions
